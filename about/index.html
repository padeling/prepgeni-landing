About PrepGeni

We're building the most effective exam prep platform for African students — starting with Nigeria's WAEC, JAMB, NECO and GCE.

PrepGeni was built by a solo technical founder who grew up watching brilliant Nigerian students fail major exams — not because they lacked ability, but because they lacked access to quality, curriculum-accurate preparation tools.

The existing options are photocopied past question booklets, YouTube videos with no interactivity, and generic quiz apps that don't understand the Nigerian curriculum. PrepGeni changes that — combining 15,250+ real exam questions with AI explanations, adaptive difficulty, and competitive features that make studying something students actually look forward to.

What Makes PrepGeni Different

Most exam prep apps are just question banks. PrepGeni is a learning system. The app tracks your accuracy per subject in real time and automatically adjusts question difficulty so you're always improving, never wasting time on questions you've already mastered.

The AI Tutor (powered by Google Gemini) gives instant explanations in plain language — not textbook jargon. The 1v1 Duel feature exists because Nigerian students are intensely competitive and learn better under pressure. Every feature was designed around how students in this context actually study.
